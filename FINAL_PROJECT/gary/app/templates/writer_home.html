The Project

We chose to create a comedy generator because we sympathized with all the people who found it hard to come up with jokes on the go. We decided to create GARY to enable users to always have a joke ready! Jokes are also a key part to all social interactions and helps keep the conversation easy-going with some amazing jokes!

We created an AI that generates jokes when the user inputs a prompt. GARY outputs a joke for the user to use themselves. GARY comes with 3 different comedians to choose from:

  • Dad Jokes
  • Patton Oswalt
  • Groot




    • The Process

    Week 1: We learned the fundamentals of Python during the first week. We learnt about the various built-in functions, loops, variables, and data types. We mainly focused on dictionaries and lists to help us prepare for the next steps. We were also introduced to various concepts within the field of Natural Language Processing to get us started with our projects.

    Week 2: We chose a project topic during the second week of AI Camp and gathered data for our comedy bot. We learned how to use Pandas and how to work with dataframes to manipulate data. We also trained our models with the datasets we had and finally had a first version of our product!

    Week 3: The final week of AI Camp, we learnt about HTML, CSS and Bootstrap. We started to design our products website and used backend tools to get our website up and running.
